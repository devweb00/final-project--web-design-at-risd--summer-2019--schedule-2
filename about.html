Hello!

My name's Grady. I'm a psychology major in Yale-NUS College minoring in computer science. I'm currently working on a capstone project aiming to use psychology to improve user experiences with rideshare apps.

Like the textbook liberal arts student, I've always been stuck somewhere between the arts and the sciences. Majoring in psychology bridged that gap for me. I realized I didn't just love designing - I also love exploring how people interact with designs, and how to make designs work well with people. I found a beautifully eclectic interest in human-computer interaction, where I could draw on everything I knew from psychology, computer science, and even art in exploring the ways our minds interact with the world outside our bodies.

Dish It!

Dish It! was a dining hall feedback app that was born in my college suite during a particularly tumultuous period in the Yale-NUS dining experience. This was my first time doing front-end development on a web app (from scratch, not to mention), so you can imagine I was very proud of it when it successfully launched.

Dish It! operated for a couple semesters, and our users preferred its simple, intuitive and accessible design over the other feedback platforms available.

The Dish It! Logo

The logo was designed to be the most elaborate visual of the app — that's how insistent we were on keeping things simple. It was versatile enough that I was able to create special logos on occasion (a la Google), which brought much life into the app.

Demo

At its height, Dish It! had nearly 200 daily votes. This video is a demo of all the features available at the time, including dietary preferences and filters, leaderboards and even theme settings.
